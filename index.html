Real Talk

QUESTIONS
ANSWERED.

Do I need sales experience?

No. Our best reps came in raw. What we can't teach is drive — if you have that, we handle the rest. Expect 2–3 weeks of real training before you're out on your own.

When do I get paid?

Weekly. Every account you close gets logged, installed, and paid out in the same cycle. No 30-day holds, no mystery deductions. You close, you get paid.

What does the schedule actually look like?

Most reps are out 5 days a week, morning to early evening. It's not a 9-to-5 — it's a performance schedule. The harder you work, the faster your day ends with something to show for it.

What's the team culture like?

Competitive but not cutthroat. We celebrate wins on the board and call out effort publicly. The top reps genuinely help the new ones — because a stronger team means better territory.

What makes D3 different from other regions?

D3 has consistently ranked #1 in Alder's regional standings for install rate and account quality. That's not luck — it's system. VLR is why.

Is there room to grow?

Yes — Danny himself came up as a rep. We have active leadership tracks for high performers. If you want to run your own team, this is how you start.
